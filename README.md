# SEPTIC SAVIOR
by Rose Hernandez and Sadie A. Ocasio Roman

## Controls

    Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
    Sprinting: hold shift while moving
    Aiming: aim with the cursor
    Shooting: hold spacebar or left click to rapid fire

## Gameplay

NORMAL MODE

    survive hordes of enemies for 3 minutes and defeat the 
    boss at the end!

ENDLESS MODE

    level up as you tear through larger and 
    larger hordes of enemies! how long can you go for?
